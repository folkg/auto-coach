About Fantasy AutoCoach

Your personal Fantasy AutoCoach will optimize and set your Yahoo fantasy lineups, automatically, every day on your behalf. You won't have to lift a finger. Just set up your team once and let it do the work for the rest of the season.

All four major sports are supported. Football, Basketball, Baseball, and Hockey, each with their own custom logic.

How it Works

  • Optimized Lineups
    The best lineup for each day is determined based on player projections, crowd-sourced player rankings, confirmed and probable starters, and injury information.

    Lineups set weekly will also take into account the schedule for each player in the upcoming week to balance games played with the other factors above.
  • Last Minute Lineup Changes
    If news comes out about a player late in the day (injury status update, starting status, etc), the lineup will be automatically updated again before each game starts to reflect the best possible roster. The changes will be made at :55 of the hour if there is an upcoming game.
  • Intelligent Use of Injury Spaces
    If there are healthy players on your IR, we will use them to fill in for a player who is injured on your starting lineup. Players will be automatically shuffled between your starting lineup and IR(+) / IL(+) / NA spaces if their eligibility allows for it.
  • Easy to Use
    There is just one option. On or Off. You can set it and forget it.

Why?

Fantasy AutoCoach was created because I wanted to spend more time enjoying the season and less time on the monotonous tasks, like shuffling my lineup.

I also wanted to create a tool that would help me win my league. I wanted to be able to focus on the strategy of the season and not have to worry about the day-to-day details. I can focus on the GM duties and let the Fantasy AutoCoach handle the rest.

Many points are lost because of poor lineup decisions, forgetting to set your lineups, or not being aware of last minute, real-life, roster moves. This tool aims to eliminate all of those needless points left stranded on the bench.

How to Set Up

Simply log in with your Yahoo credentials and all of your teams will be presented to you. Just set the toggle to "On" for each team and you're done. You can always come back to turn it off for any team if you'd prefer to work them manually.

What it Won't Do

Fantasy AutoCoach will not make trades, adds, drops, or waiver wire pickups for you (yet..). It will not make any permanent roster decisions for you. It will just optimize your lineup every day.
