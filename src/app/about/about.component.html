About The Fantasy Autopilot

Fantasy Autopilot will optimize and set your fantasy lineups automatically every day on your behalf. You won't have to lift a finger. Just set up your team once and let it do the work for the rest of the season.

How it Works

  • Optimize Lineups
    The best lineup for each day (or week) is determined based on player projections, crowd-sourced player rankings, announced starters, and injury information.
  • Last Minute Lineup Changes
    If a player is injured or announced as a starter late in the day, the lineup will be automatically updated again before each game starts. The changes will be made at :55 of the hour if there is an upcoming game.
  • Intelligent Use of Injury Spaces
    If there are healthy players on your IR, we will use them to fill in for a player who is injured on your starting lineup. Players will be automatically shuffled between your starting lineup and IR / IR+ / NA spaces if their eligibility allows for it.
  • Easy to Use
    There is just one option. On or Off. You can set it and forget it.

Why?

Fantasy Autopilot was created because I was tired of having to manually set my lineups every day. I wanted to spend more time enjoying the season and less time on the monotonous tasks.

I also wanted to create a tool that would help me win my league. I wanted to be able to focus on the strategy of the season and not have to worry about the day-to-day minutiae. Many points are lost because of poor lineup decisions, forgetting to set your lineups, or not being aware of last minute, real-life, roster moves. This tool aims to eliminate all of those needless points stranded on the bench.

How to Set Up

Just log in with your Yahoo credentials and all of your teams will be presented to you. Just set the toggle to "On" and you're done. You can always come back set the toggle to "Off" if you want to turn it off for a particular team.

What it Won't Do

Fantasy Autopilot will not make trades, adds, drops, or waiver wire pickups for you. It will not make any roster decisions for you. It will just optimize your lineup every day.

Upcoming Features

Here are some of the things that are coming up:

  • Fill Rosters Positions for Injured Players
    If you have a player on your roster who is injured, we will move them to an open IR / IR+ / NA slot if space permits and fill that roster position with a healthy player from the free agency pool or waivers.
  • Suggest Add and Drop Candidate Players
    We will suggest players to add and players drop based on their projected performance, hot streaks, the moves of other owners, and your team's needs.
